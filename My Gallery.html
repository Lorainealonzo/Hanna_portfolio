About

Living Art is a gallery that presents a collection of images showing the beauty of nature, moments from everyday life, modern architectural designs, and colorful abstract artwork. Each image highlights different forms of creativity and visual expression that can inspire viewers and capture their attention. This gallery was created as part of my Human–Computer Interaction activity to demonstrate how images can be arranged clearly using a simple layout, proper spacing, and easy navigation. The goal of this website is to provide a clean and organized viewing experience that makes the content easy to explore and understand. Through this gallery, users can appreciate different perspectives of art while experiencing a user-friendly webpage design.
